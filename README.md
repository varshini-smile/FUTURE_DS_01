#FUTURE_DS_01#

Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance.


**Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard**

**Project Overview**

This project analyzes online retail sales data using Excel and Power BI to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, customer activity, and regional sales performance. The objective is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision-making.


---

**Data Cleaning Process (Excel)**

The dataset was cleaned before analysis using the following steps:

Removed rows with blank Customer IDs

Removed rows with blank Product Descriptions

Removed cancelled transactions (Invoice numbers starting with "C")

Removed records with negative or zero Quantity

Removed records with negative or zero Unit Price

Removed duplicate records

Created a Sales column (Quantity × Unit Price)

Created a Month column for monthly trend analysis

Converted the dataset into an Excel Table for easier analysis

**Business Questions & Answers**

1. Which products generate the most revenue?

The highest revenue-generating products are:

1. PAPER CRAFT , LITTLE BIRDIE


2. REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER


3. WHITE HANGING HEART T-LIGHT HOLDER


4. JUMBO BAG RED RETROSPOT


5. MEDIUM CERAMIC TOP STORAGE JAR



These products contribute a significant share of total sales revenue and play a key role in overall business performance.


---

2. How do sales change over time?

Sales fluctuate across different months.

Key observations from the monthly sales trend analysis:

Some months show strong revenue growth.

Certain periods experience lower sales, indicating seasonal variations.

Sales generally increase during peak shopping periods.

Customer demand changes throughout the year, affecting revenue patterns.


This trend analysis helps businesses plan inventory, promotions, and marketing activities more effectively.


---

3. Which regions are the most profitable?

The most profitable region identified from the analysis is:

United Kingdom

Key observations:

The United Kingdom contributes the largest share of total sales revenue.

Revenue from the UK significantly exceeds that of other countries.

Several other countries contribute to sales, but at a much smaller scale.


This indicates that the UK is the primary market for the business.


---

4. Where should the business focus to grow faster?

Based on the dashboard insights, the business should focus on:

Increasing inventory for top-selling products.

Expanding marketing efforts in high-performing countries.

Running promotional campaigns during low-sales periods.

Improving customer acquisition in underperforming regions.

Strengthening customer retention strategies.


These actions can help improve revenue growth and business performance.


---

**Key Insights**

The United Kingdom generates the highest revenue among all countries.

A small group of products contributes a large percentage of total sales.

Sales patterns vary throughout the year, indicating seasonal demand.

Revenue is concentrated in a few high-performing regions.

Top-selling products have a significant impact on overall business success.



---

**Recommendations**

Maintain sufficient inventory levels for high-demand products.

Focus marketing investments on top-performing regions.

Introduce targeted promotions during weaker sales periods.

Expand customer outreach in regions with growth potential.

Continuously monitor sales trends to improve business decisions.
